Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours

How do I book a tour?

Select your desired package and click "Book Now" to proceed with the booking process. You'll need to provide traveler details and payment information to complete your reservation.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Yes, we offer customizable packages for most destinations. Contact our travel consultants to discuss your specific requirements and we'll create a personalized itinerary just for you.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by package. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund, while cancellations within 30 days may incur fees. Please refer to the specific package details or contact us for exact terms.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for groups of 6 or more people traveling together. Contact our group travel specialists for customized quotes and special arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and digital payment methods like PayPal. Some locations may also accept cash payments.
