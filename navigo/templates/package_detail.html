Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the tour package price?

The package includes all accommodations in 4-star hotels, daily breakfast, 3 gourmet dinners, all transportation during the tour, entrance fees to all attractions mentioned in the itinerary, professional guide services, and airport transfers.

What's not included in the package?

International flights, travel insurance, visa fees, personal expenses, optional activities, and meals not specified in the itinerary are not included. Gratuities for guides and drivers are at your discretion.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances.

What should I pack for this tour?

We recommend comfortable walking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best), a daypack, sunscreen, sunglasses, camera, travel adapters, and any personal medications. A detailed packing list will be provided after booking.
